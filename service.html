Our Services

Holistic Digital Solutions & Career Acceleration Programs

Mobile App Development

We design and develop scalable mobile applications for Android, iOS, and hybrid platforms. From UI/UX design to backend systems, our mobile solutions are crafted to deliver seamless user experiences, boost retention, and drive growth. Whether it's a startup MVP or an enterprise-level product, we align development with business strategy.

Web Development

Our web development team delivers performance-oriented solutions—from static websites to advanced web portals. We focus on building secure, SEO-optimized, and mobile-friendly platforms using modern stacks like React, Node.js, and Laravel. We prioritize speed, maintainability, and user-first design across all screen sizes.

AI Solutions & Automation

We build intelligent systems that drive automation, analytics, and decision-making. Our offerings include AI-based billing tools, chatbots, predictive models, and custom ML pipelines. Whether you’re streamlining operations or building smart user features, our AI services are crafted to deliver real-world impact, not just theory.

Software Development Training

We offer deep-dive training programs in programming, frontend/backend development, and DevOps. Courses are led by real developers from top mnc companies, who guide learners through hands-on projects and real-world coding challenges. You’ll learn not just how to write code—but how to build systems that scale and deliver value.

Software Testing & QA Training

Our QA training equips students with expertise in manual, automation, and performance testing. You'll explore industry tools like Selenium, JIRA, Postman, and Jenkins. With expert-led sessions and real projects, you’ll gain the confidence and skills needed to catch bugs, ensure quality, and accelerate releases in real development pipelines.

Online Internship Program

Asyncra’s internships offer a practical edge—participants contribute to real client projects under mentor supervision. You’ll learn agile workflows, project communication, and code versioning while building your portfolio. Interns gain feedback, improve collaboration skills, and receive certification that adds real weight to resumes.

Mentors from Top Tech Companies

We partner with experienced professionals from Top tech companies to mentor our learners. These mentors spend hours weekly providing insights, guidance, and best practices—bridging the gap between what’s taught in school and what’s expected in the tech industry.

1-on-1 Mentorship Program

Every learner is unique—our 1-on-1 mentoring ensures personalized learning journeys. Whether it’s clarifying doubts, career planning, or deep technical reviews, our mentors provide undivided attention to help learners achieve clarity, mastery, and direction at their own pace.

Career Guidance & Industry Prep

We help students plan and pivot their careers effectively—whether they’re starting out or switching fields. Our experts assist with resume building, job portal optimization, mock interviews, and soft skill training, ensuring you enter the job market with confidence and clarity.

College Project Support

We assist students in ideation, architecture, and execution of impactful academic projects. From code reviews to final-year presentations, we help you turn your ideas into clean, innovative software backed by industry-ready practices.

Job-Ready Certification Tracks

Each course and internship is tied to a certification issued by Asyncra Technologies, recognized by our partner companies. These certifications aren't just documents—they reflect your real experience, your learning path, and your practical contributions to projects and codebases.
